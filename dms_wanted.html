Green Dragon Inn

Professionals Wanted

Dugeon Masters listed on Green Dragon Inn are held to a +5 standard:

  • You have a teacher's heart with a well of infinite patience
  • You are more entertaining than a bard and really know how to engage a crowd
  • You set a high standard of excellence and make sure players end the session happy

Dungeon Masters go through a trial period and must agree to our "All Characters Welcome" policy.

Step 1: Apply

Fill out our form to provide basic details. We'll follow up with a 30 minute conversation including onboarding.

Step 2: Trial period

There is no membership fee for new DMs but there is also a discount for players. Once your services have been reviewed you become a full member.

Step 3: Do what you love

Book play sessions and entertain 100s. As a full member of Green Dragon Inn you have access to all the perks and benefits of a constant pipeline of clients.

Take the initiative. Players need you.
